About Kondaji Chivda

Bringing Authentic & Crunchy Delights to Your Plate

Our Story

Kondaji Chivda is a well-known brand in Nashik, Maharashtra, India. They have been manufacturing chivda since 1920.

Kondaji Chivda is a legacy of taste and tradition. With years of experience in making the finest chivda, we use premium ingredients to create a crispy and flavorful snack loved by everyone.

Our mission is to bring authentic Maharashtrian flavors to every home, ensuring high-quality and taste in every bite. We believe in purity, tradition, and customer satisfaction.
